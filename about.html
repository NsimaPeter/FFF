    goals and objectives

    To be an established club within Swedish elite football with as high sporting results as possible without risking the association's finances or credibility. We must work to maximize the sporting results of our own team as well as to continue to develop football individuals for the benefit of football both nationally and internationally.

    Be the entire Falkenberg association. The pride of all Falkenbergers. Give Falkenberg elite football at the national top level as well as a meaningful leisure activity for children and young people.
